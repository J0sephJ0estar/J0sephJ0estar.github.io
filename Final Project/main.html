Final Project: Ray Tracing

Description:

      Create your own scene using ray tracing algorithm.
      You may choose to draw half of your scene by yourself and the other half by using openGL, then merge them into a whole one to compare with each other, but you can also draw a whole scene by one method.

Design Issues:

      Only draw a single scene here because I just don't konw how to merge it with openGL rendered ones.
      Code Design Issues: To describe the direction of a piece of light and the norm vector of an object, a 3d vector is needed, so there's a point class. Also, there's a material class to restore different reflection properties of different objects made of different materials and a ray class to define the starting position of a light and provide functions to check whether a light is intersected with an object
      Ray Casting Issues: Each ray must be tested for intersection with some subset of all the objects in the scene. Once the nearest object has been identified, the algorithm will estimate the incoming light at the point of intersection, examine the material properties of the object, and combine this information to calculate the final color of the pixel. Phong illumination is used here.
